Brief

Dong Liu received the B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, China, in 2004 and 2009, respectively. He was a Member of Research Staff with Nokia Research Center, Beijing, China, from 2009 to 2012. He joined USTC in 2012 and became a Professor in 2020.
His research interests include image and video processing, coding, analysis, and data mining. He has authored or co-authored more than 100 papers in international journals and conferences, which were cited more than 7000 times according to Google Scholar (h-index is 35). He has more than 20 granted patents. He has several technique proposals adopted by standardization groups. He received the 2009 IEEE TCSVT Best Paper Award and the VCIP 2016 Best 10% Paper Award. He and his students were winners of several technical challenges held in ICCV 2019, ACM MM 2019, ACM MM 2018, ECCV 2018, CVPR 2018, and ICME 2016. He is a Senior Member of IEEE, CCF, and CSIG, a Member of ACM, an elected member of MSA-TC of IEEE CAS Society, and an elected member of Multimedia TC of CSIG. He serves or had served as the Chair of IEEE 1857.11 Standard Working Subgroup (previously known as FVC-SG), an Associate Editor for Frontiers in Signal Processing, an Organizing Committee Member for VCIP 2022, ChinaMM 2022, ICMR 2021, ICME 2021, ICME 2019, etc. He had organized several special sessions/workshop/challenges in ICME, FG, ICIP, VCIP, MMSP, MIPR, and ChinaMM.
